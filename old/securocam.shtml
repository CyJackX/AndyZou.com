SECUROCAM3000

Securocam3000 is the story of Xander McGuff and Ace Johnson, two clueless employees of the all-seeing Big Brother state in the year 2222!

Co-written, Co-produced, and Co-Directed by Andy Zou and Jay Eisenberg.
