Who, or what, is Andy Zou?

Andy is a jack-of-all-trades, multi-hyphenate, Renaissance guy of digital content! A director, cinematographer, editor, writer, and sometimes coder, Andy has worked on hundreds of short and long-form projects over the past decade, establishing a reputation amongst the local entertainment scene as a go-to resource for turnkey video and content production. Past clients have included Barkbox, Snapchat, Color of Change, Comedy Central, The Architect's Newspaper, MOMA, and countless others.

Andy graduated from New York University's Experimental Theatre program with a BFA in Drama and a minor in Mathematics & Computer Science.
