About

Andy is a jack-of-all-trades Renaissance guy of creative digital content. A director, cinematographer, editor, writer, and sometimes coder, Andy has worked on hundreds of short and long-form projects over the past decade, establishing a reputation as a go-to guy for turnkey video and content production. Past clients have included Comedy Central, Snapchat, Color of Change, Barkbox, Clickhole, The Architect's Newspaper, MOMA, and many others.

Andy graduated from New York University's Experimental Theatre program with a BFA in Drama and a Minor in Mathematics & Computer Science.
